About Me

I approach embedded systems as complete engineering ecosystems—where hardware, firmware, signal integrity, and real-time behavior must work together reliably under constraints.

My work focuses on building robust embedded platforms that move from prototype to deployable systems through iterative testing, firmware optimization, and system-level integration. I enjoy solving problems close to the hardware layer—debugging interfaces, processing sensor data, validating communication pipelines, and improving system reliability in dynamic environments.
