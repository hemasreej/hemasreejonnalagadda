Why 90% of C++ Features Are Not Used in Aerospace

Languages like Java, Python, and C# use a garbage collector — automatic memory cleanup that runs in the background and can stall execution unpredictably. Flight-critical systems must respond in real time, every time, with guaranteed timing — which is why GC-based languages are banned in safety-critical code, and why aerospace teams rely on a strict, minimal subset of C++.
